About Eyebrowse

Eyebrowse aims to create a social outdoors for your web browsing.

Managing your whitelist

Using the browser plugin that we've created for Chrome, it will occasionally ask you while you are browsing the Internet whether you would like to whitelist the domain that you are currently on. You can also add a domain to your whitelist without being prompted by clicking on the Eyebrowse logo and selecting 'Whitelist this Domain'. You can always add more domains or remove domains from your whitelist by editing your profile on the Eyebrowse website. Once you've added a domain to your whitelist that that you are comfortable sharing your activity on, the plugin will track when you visit a page on that domain and publish it to your Eyebrowse feed for you automatically. If you want to mark a visit to a webpage just this one time without putting the domain in your whitelist, you can do this from within the Eyebrowse plugin by clicking "Mark a visit to this page" while on that page.

Social activities in the plugin

If you click on the Eyebrowse logo in your Chrome browser, you can also post a Scribble, which is a short message or comment of your choosing, to that particular page. Scribbles will also post to your Eyebrowse feed and then the home feeds of your followers. You can think of Scribbles as being similar to a news article comment, Facebook post, or Twitter post. The Eyebrowse plugin also supports more light-weight discussions with people on the page in the chat window below the Scribble feed. These discussions, while public, do not get posted to your feed, and people can only see them when they are on that page. In the plugin, you can also see who else is on the same page and the same domain as you at that moment.

Social activities on the website

On the Eyebrowse website, your homepage is a feed of all the people you are following. You can see the webpages that they've visited on their whitelist, and you can click through to view those webpages and discover interesting content. You can also view the Firehose of all Eyebrowse visits and search within it to find content or new people to follow. You can browse other people's profile pages and view people's followers and followees. Finally, you can edit your own profile and manage your whitelist.

About Us

We are a team of researchers and students at MIT CSAIL and members of the Haystack Research Group at MIT. Our overall research goal is to make it easier for people to collect, organize, find, visualize, and share information. Eyebrowse is pursuing this goal for web activity and traversal.

Thank you for using Eyebrowse!
Eyebrowse is a research project from MIT CSAIL's Haystack Group exploring the possibilities of social web browsing.

Eyebrowse is a tool that allows you to choose to share information with others using the Eyebrowse tool while you are browsing the web, including information like certain sites or pages that you visit, comments that you leave using the Eyebrowse tool, or tags that you annotate web pages with. None of your browsing activity is shared unless you actively choose to whitelist a domain.

Many of the social browsing features of Eyebrowse are not relevant to the online news research study you are about to take part in. You may choose to use, or not use, those features.

Guarantees to you as a participant in this research project:
- Your participation is voluntary. You may uninstall Eyebrowse and abandon the application at any time.
- Please email eyebrowse@mit.edu with any concerns or questions.
